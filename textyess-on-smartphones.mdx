Quickly access your TextYess conversations, orders, and revenue by adding it to your smartphone's home screen. Here's how:

## For iPhone Users

- **Step 1:** Open Safari and navigate to the TextYess app.
- **Step 2:** Tap the share button, then choose "Add to Home Screen".
- **Step 3:** If needed, use "Edit Shares" to find this option.
- **Step 4:** Rename if desired and tap "Add".

## For Android Users

- **Step 1:** Use any browser (e.g., Google Chrome) to visit the TextYess site.
- **Step 2:** Tap the menu (three dots), select "Add to Home Screen".
- **Step 3:** Rename as desired, then tap "Add."

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/add_to_home_screen_android" />
  </Frame>

This creates a shortcut on your home screen, allowing easy management just like any other app.
