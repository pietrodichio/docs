---
title: "How to manually manage conversations"
description: "Once a client has started a conversation, TextYess provides the ability to manage it manually."
---

If you wish to disable AI in a specific conversation and respond manually, simply open the conversation and click on the button next to **_AI Active_**:

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/conversation.png" />
</Frame>

After clicking on the button, you can directly send the message you want to your customer.

<Warning>
  Remember that you will only be able to send messages to customers who have
  last sent you a message within a time window of no more than **24 hours**.
</Warning>
