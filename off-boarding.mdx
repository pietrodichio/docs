Title: Off-boarding flow
---

In order to complete your unsubscription process, you should do an important action: **delete your phone number on which you installed our AI-Assistant** (or the number we provided you with) **from your Facebook Business Manager.**

<Steps>
  <Step>Go to your Business Settings Page</Step>
  <Step>Choose the Facebook Business account (1)</Step>
  <Step>Click on WhatsApp Accounts (2) under the Accounts section</Step>
  <Step>Choose the WhatsApp account (3)</Step>
  <Step>
    Select Settings (4) then WhatsApp Manager (5)
    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/offboarding.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Click on Phone Numbers (1) under Account Tools
    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/manager.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Press the Delete icon next to the phone number you want to delete.
    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/delete-connected.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Then click on Next

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/delete.png" />
    </Frame>
